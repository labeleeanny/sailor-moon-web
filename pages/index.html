FUNNY FACTS

The hand signs that Sailor Moon makes while she says her famous line, “In the name of the moon, I will punish you” means "I love you” in sign language.

The creator of Sailor Moon was inspired by the Power Rangers.

Sailor Moon was actually supposed to have pink or silver hair after her transformation.

In the original manga series, Sailor Venus was cursed so that she would never find the love of her life.

Sailor Mercury isn't technically allergic to love or love letters — her hives could just be a stress response.

In the Polish translation of the manga series, Sailor Saturn was mistakenly translated as "SAILOR SATAN".

Disney once planned to secure the rights to Sailor Moon, in order to make a feature film.
